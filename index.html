About Us

Has your significant other finally popped the question? Are you trying to start planning your big day and really do not know where to start? Wedding Belles is your answer. This product is every bride and groom's tool to help them successfully plan and coordinate their wedding day. Created in 2010 by Emily White, Wedding Belles gives you the power to plan every little detail about your day, right down to the colour harmonies. This tool keeps every thing in one place, from guest list to photographs of the venue and even the ability to video chat with a wedding planner, and is available on any and every device for easy planning and efficiency that's accessible by both bride and groom, anywhere! Give Wedding Belles a try today & make your own happily ever after.
